Welcome to Bw Jin’s website! I am a PHD student in Beijing Computation Science Research Center. My research lies in the simulation on animals’ swimming and proposing some novel ideas on design of swimming robot. I am now studying the unique swimming style(figure-of-8) of larva of midge and analyzing the swimming mechanics of larva. Besides, I am also interested in swimming optimization. I am now trying to adjust the swimming gait of robot when the joint does not work using reinforcement learning. If you are interested in my work, you can contact with me by mail(jinbowen@csrc.ac.cn) and we can talk later! Thank you!
